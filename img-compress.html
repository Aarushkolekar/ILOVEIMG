How to Use Our Image Compression Tool

  1. Click "Browse Files" or drag and drop your image into the upload area
  2. Adjust the compression level using the slider (higher = better quality, larger file)
  3. Select your desired output format (JPEG, PNG, WebP, or keep original)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your image
  5. Preview the compressed version and download when satisfied

Why Compress Images?

  • Faster Website Loading: Smaller images load faster, improving user experience
  • Save Storage Space: Reduce the amount of storage needed for your images
  • Bandwidth Savings: Use less data when uploading or sharing images online
  • SEO Benefits: Faster websites rank higher in search results
